The Rules
01
3 Levels
‚ñæ
  • ‚óèEvery market move comes in 3 pushes (levels)
  • ‚óèMove ends with M (top) or W (bottom)
  • ‚óèM and W can disguise as other patterns (H&S, harmonics, etc.)
  • ‚óèWe trade REVERSALS ‚Äî catching tops and bottoms after 3 levels complete
02
Vector Candle + Volume
‚ñæ
  • ‚óèLevel 3 ends with the biggest candle and highest volume (climax)
  • ‚óèCreates extreme fear (selling) or greed (buying) ‚Äî THE TRAP
  • ‚óèMarket makers NEED this panic to fill their orders on the other side
  • ‚óèThe crowd sees danger. We see opportunity.
03
Wait for 2nd Leg of M/W
‚ñæ
  • ‚óèDon't enter on first touch. Wait for W (or M) to complete
  • ‚óè2nd leg retests the area on weaker momentum = confirmation
  • ‚óèFirst leg = capitulation. Bounce = relief. Second leg = the test.
04
Channel
‚ñæ
  • ‚óèDraw a channel on the move (descending for downtrend, ascending for uptrend)
  • ‚óèPrice stays inside channel during the trend
  • ‚óèEnter only AFTER price breaks out of the channel
  • ‚óèChannel break = confirmation reversal is real, not just a bounce
05
Wait for Stop Hunt
‚ñæ
  • ‚óèAfter first channel break, price often comes BACK into channel for 2nd leg of W
  • ‚óèThis sweeps stop losses of early entries ‚Üí market makers collect liquidity
  • ‚óèWait for the stop hunt to complete before entering
  • ‚óèNova's advantage: patience costs nothing when monitoring 50 setups
06
Check the Daily
‚ñæ
  • ‚óèAlways align with the higher timeframe
  • ‚óèIf Daily says UP ‚Üí only look for W bottoms (longs) on lower TFs
  • ‚óèIf Daily says DOWN ‚Üí only look for M tops (shorts) on lower TFs
  • ‚óèNever counter the big trend. Daily = direction, lower TF = entry.
08
Vector Candle Exit ‚òÖ V5.2 EXIT RULE
‚ñæ
  • ‚óèExit on exhaustion candle in trade direction: body > 1.2√ó average, volume > 1.5√ó average
  • ‚óèThe same signal that marks the START of a reversal also marks the END of a move
  • ‚óèNo fixed R target ‚Äî let the market tell you when the move is exhausted
  • ‚óèCaptures the full move on strong trends, exits early on weak ones
  • ‚óèBacktested at +115.88R across 87 trades ‚Äî best of all versions
